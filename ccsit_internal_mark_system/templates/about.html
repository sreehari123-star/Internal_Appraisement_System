ABOUT CCSIT

Centre for Computer Science and Information Technology

Know More

CCSIT(Centre for Computer Science and Information Technology) is the subsidiary section of the university of calicut providing technical courses mainly computer science and computer application courses within the 12 centres across kerala under the supervision of University of Calicut. Post graduation courses are mainly provided by the centres with well qualified teachers and staffs for the proper functioning of the institution.The pg courses offered here is a two year post graduation courses with the certificate from the Calicut University. The CCSIT Center has one institute in the University Campus itself with hostel and campus facilities provided by CU.
